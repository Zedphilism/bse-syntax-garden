### IDEEA

Accurate digital representation of analog signals requires sampling at a sufficient rate defined by Nyquist’s theorem.

### Details

- **Sampling**
    - Taking discrete measurements of analog signal
- **Nyquist Theorem**
    - Sampling frequency ≥ 2 × signal frequency
- If violated:
    - Aliasing occurs (distortion)
- Real-world example:
    - Audio recording (44.1 kHz)
