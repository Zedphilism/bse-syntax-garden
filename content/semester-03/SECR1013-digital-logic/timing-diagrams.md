### IDEEA

Timing diagrams visualize how multiple digital signals change over time and interact with each other.

### Details

- Shows:
    - Multiple signals (A, B, C)
    - Their HIGH/LOW states over time
- Used to:
    - Analyze system behavior
    - Debug circuits
- Example:
    - At time t:
        - A = 1, B = 0, C = 1
- Important for:
    - Sequential logic
    - Clock synchronization
