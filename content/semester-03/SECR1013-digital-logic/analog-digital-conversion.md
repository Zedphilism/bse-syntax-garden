### IDEEA

Real-world signals must be converted between analog and digital forms for processing and output.

### Details

- **ADC (Analog to Digital Converter)**
    - Converts real-world signals → digital
    - Steps:
        - Sampling
        - Quantization
        - Encoding
    - Loss of accuracy can occur
- **DAC (Digital to Analog Converter)**
    - Converts digital → analog
    - Needed for:
        - Speakers
        - Audio output
- Example system:
    - CD player:
        - Reads digital → converts to analog → plays sound
