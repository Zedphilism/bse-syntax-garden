### IDEEA

Digital systems represent information using binary digits (bits) mapped to physical voltage levels.

### Details

- **Bit (binary digit)**
    - Smallest unit of data
    - Values: 0 or 1
- **Voltage representation**
    - HIGH → 1
    - LOW → 0
- There is also an **invalid region** between HIGH and LOW
- Important concept:
    - Logic ≠ number, it’s a **state**
- Used in:
    - CPUs
    - Memory
    - Digital circuits
