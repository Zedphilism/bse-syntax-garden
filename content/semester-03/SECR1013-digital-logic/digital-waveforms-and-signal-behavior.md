### IDEEA

Digital signals are represented as waveforms consisting of transitions between HIGH and LOW voltage levels over time.

### Details

- Digital waveform = series of pulses
- Types:
    - **Periodic** → repeats (clock signals)
    - **Non-periodic** → irregular
- Key components:
    - Rising edge (LOW → HIGH)
    - Falling edge (HIGH → LOW)
- Important terms:
    - **Period (T)** → time for one cycle
    - **Frequency (f)** → cycles per second
        - f = 1/T
- Used in:
    - Clock signals
    - Timing control
